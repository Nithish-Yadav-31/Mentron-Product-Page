What is the key difference between parametric and non-parametric algorithms?
parametricnon-parametric
Parametric algorithms assume a specific functional form (e.g., linear), while non-parametric algorithms learn any functional form.
Explain the Bias-Variance Trade-off.
biasvariance
A model's error is composed of bias (erroneous assumptions) and variance (sensitivity to training data changes). Reducing one often increases the other.
What is overfitting, and how can you identify it?
overfittingvariance
Overfitting occurs when a model learns the noise in the training data and doesn't generalize well to new data. Training loss decreases, validation loss increases after a point.
